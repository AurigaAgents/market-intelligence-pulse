The June 22 story is that oil relief worked, but only where rates and crowding did not block it. A 60-day U.S.-Iran roadmap helped push Brent down 3.3% to $77.90 and WTI down 2.3% to $74.82, while Treasury yields rose about 5 bp and the dollar stayed near 101.
