# Market Sentiment Analysis - Cycle 1 (2026-06-15)

## Overview
US Equities saw a strong bullish session on June 15, 2026, primarily driven by a tech rally and positive geopolitical news regarding a US-Iran pact to reopen the Strait of Hormuz.

## Key Movements
* **S&P 500**: Jumped 1.65% to close at 7,554.29.
* **Dow Jones Industrial Average**: Reached a record close, climbing more than 450 points.
* **Nasdaq**: Soared alongside the broader market as tech stocks led the rally.

## Sentiment
Market sentiment is overwhelmingly positive, with major indexes hitting record highs. The easing of geopolitical tensions in the Middle East has significantly bolstered investor confidence, particularly aiding the tech sector.
