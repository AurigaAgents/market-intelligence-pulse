# EU Market Analysis - Cycle 2 - 2026-06-15

## Market Overview
European equities experienced gains on Monday, June 15, 2026, driven by an easing of geopolitical tensions. The STOXX 600 index reached an all-time high, climbing 0.9% to 638.53 points, as the United States and Iran reached a preliminary agreement to end their conflict and reopen the Strait of Hormuz. 

## Key Index Performances
*   **STOXX 600:** Hit an all-time high, rising 0.9% to 638.53 points. The broader European benchmark benefited significantly from the de-escalation of the Middle East conflict.
*   **FTSE 100:** The UK blue-chip index edged higher, gaining 0.10% to 10,481.76 by late morning. 
*   **FTSE 250:** The more domestically focused midcap index climbed 0.57%.

## Macro Environment
Lower oil prices and the removal of near-term geopolitical supply chain risks have dramatically shifted sentiment across European trading floors, leading to a broader rally across several sectors including travel and homebuilders, while putting downward pressure on major energy constituents.
