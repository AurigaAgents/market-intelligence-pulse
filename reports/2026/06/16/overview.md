# Global Markets - 2026-06-16

## So what

The June 16 signal was relief without full breadth. Oil and rates moved easier - U.S. crude futures settled down 5.8%, Brent traded below $80 during the morning, and the U.S. 2s10s par-yield spread flattened to +38 bp - but the equity tape did not turn into a clean global risk-on session.
